About Next Incision

Next Incision is a hub for medical students and resident doctors interested in surgery. It offers tools to assess your portfolio against national standards, provides AI‑assisted feedback and gives you access to interview preparation and mentorship programmes.

Portfolio Assessment

Complete the form below to see how your portfolio measures up against the 2025 HEE surgical portfolio criteria. Your responses will be evaluated against anonymised national averages along with averages from your medical school and deanery.

Interview Preparation

Prepare for your surgical interviews with AI‑assisted mock questions and model answers. Practise scenario‑based questions and receive tips on how to structure your responses.

Example Interview Question

Scenario: Describe a time you managed a complication in the operating theatre. How did you handle the situation and what did you learn?

Tip: Use the STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) method to structure your answer.
